## 1. Famewall ($1,000/month)

![successful micro saas famewall](/images/blog/2024-07-famewall-homepage-microsaas-example-of-a-success-story-1024x470.png)

- **Time to Achieve MRR**: 12 months

-**Target Audience**: Website owners

-**Key Feature**: Collect and display testimonials easily

[**Famewall**](https://famewall.io/) is a micro SaaS that enables website owners to easily collect and display testimonials. Users receive a link to a testimonial collection page where customers can write or record their feedback. 

Famewall then turns these testimonials into embeddable widgets in a variety of display options. 

If that sounds simple, it’s because it is! 

## 2. Clickpilot ($1,600/month)

![successful micro saas ](/images/blog/2024-07-clickpilot-homepage-1024x565.png)

-**Time to Achieve MRR**: 5 months

-**Target Audience**: YouTube content creators

-**Key Feature**: Compare YouTube thumbnails against competitors

[**Clickpilot**](https://clickpilot.app/) is a micro app that allows users to preview and compare their YouTube video thumbnails against different YouTube interfaces, swap out different versions of thumbnails to find the most effective one, and auto-generate different versions of titles. 

Comparing thumbnails against competitors or those in specific search results is very useful for content creators, especially at the reasonable price of $10 a month. 

**Cool fact:**Clickpilot reached $1,600 MRR in just five months while costing only $100 to run!

## 3. GrowthPanels ($2,000/month)

![successful micro saas growthpanels](/images/blog/2024-07-growthpanels-saas-example-1024x658.png)

- **Time to Achieve MRR**: 2 months

-**Target Audience**: SaaS founders

-**Key Feature**: Reward customers for specific actions

[**GrowthPanels**](https://growthpanels.com/) helps other SaaS founders reward customers with discounts for taking specific actions, such as posting about a product or referring a friend. 

Founders set actions and rewards through GrowthPanels and embed it on their site, and customers who carry out the actions enjoy discounts on their next Stripe bill. 

## 4. Sketch Logo AI ($3,100/month)

![successful micro saas sketch logo ai](/images/blog/2024-07-sketchlogo-ai-homepage-1024x462.png)

-**Time to Achieve MRR**: 4 months

-**Target Audience**: Business owners and designers

-**Key Feature**: AI-generated logo designs from sketches

[**Sketch Logo AI**](https://www.sketchlogo.ai/) allows users to create really cool-looking designs using AI, and in a world saturated with products like this, we***really***mean it!

Users draw on a sketchpad, provide a written prompt, and the AI generates beautiful designs that can be further refined with additional prompts.

## 5. Repurposepie ($5,000/month)

![successful micro saas repurposepie](/images/blog/2024-07-repurposepie-homepage-1024x455.png)

[**RepurposePie**](https://repurposepie.io/) automatically converts tweets into short videos for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and similar platforms, with Instagram support coming soon. 

There’s not much else to say - it’s a very pure interpretation of a micro SaaS.

## 6. Unicorn Platform ($16,000/month)

![successful micro saas unicorn platform](/images/blog/2024-07-unicorn-platform-incroduvble-micro-ssaas-success-story-1024x466.png)

- **Time to Achieve MRR**: Not stated

-**Target Audience**: Startups and solopreneurs

-**Key Feature**: AI website builder for simple use cases

[**Unicorn Platform**](https://unicornplatform.com/) is an AI website builder pitched as a simpler alternative to WordPress or Webflow to appeal to startups and solopreneurs who prioritize ease of use and speed over complexity at the cost of steeper learning curves.

It was also [**acquired for $800,000 by MarsX**](https://theygotacquired.com/saas/unicorn-platform-acquired-by-mars/), a platform combining micro apps to help create no code and low code apps. 

![make money with micro saas unicorn platform](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfCsL8GIgn6e6Wapg7SmSouxpDTpTNXt0yxyYtzOmz80yrCuqtIYhhx8MewpBK_-wYCfvJSEM2zGseqejHjV5ThPGpWOTkNfP1HbyRTUDNpf9Hu0z_1JaLorE1lx254BI_5Yu5SN3YAtbJTktU8OONiWq1U?key=HbHipk5ctKGJk14iSGyo7w)
