## Our BIGGEST SaaS Ideas List Yet!

We usually cherry-pick SaaS ideas when making a list, but this time, we’re giving you everything!

_EVERYTHING!_

Here are **30 real SaaS ideas** that you can steal, borrow, [**build, and grow an app in 2025**](https://upstackstudio.com/blog/how-to-create-an-app/). 

These are**real, actionable ideas** from the front pages of the internet. We’ve grouped them based on target market, and we’re going to speedrun the list, covering:

-**Name** of the SaaS

-\*\*What it does

-\*\*How it benefits users

-**Difficulty to build** (on a scale of 1 to 10)

Let’s kick things off with our favorite target market…

## Businesses & Enterprises

### ColdConnector

Difficulty: 5/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-coldconnector-homepage-1024x470.png)

**What it does:**[**Personalizes cold emails with AI**](https://coldconnector.com/) by researching target companies to include lead values, needs, and challenges.

**Benefit:** Increases conversion rates by making emails more relevant.

### DeliveryMan AI

Difficulty: 7/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-deliveryai-homepage-1024x514.png)

**What it does:** [**Automates cold emails by generating multiple subdomains**](https://deliveryman.ai/) to send high volumes of emails while bypassing spam filters.

**Benefit:** Perfect for founders and startups with minimal reputational risks.

### Stackdeck

Difficulty: 10/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-stackdeck-homepage-1024x496.png)

**What it does:** [**A SaaS management tool**](https://stackdeck.com/) that provides insights into software subscriptions, cost-saving opportunities, and renewal deadlines.

**Benefit:** Optimizes SaaS spending for businesses.

### Totals App

Difficulty: 3/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-totals-app-homepage-1024x520.png)

**What it does:** A MacOS-only service providing [**real-time revenue updates**](https://totalsapp.com/) from multiple sources directly in the menubar.

**Benefit:** Offers a lightweight, hassle-free experience for business owners.

### OctoIQ

Difficulty: 5/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-octoiq-homepage-1024x470.png)

**What it does:** [**An AI proposal management tool**](https://octoiq.io/) that simplifies information gathering for proposals.

**Benefit:** Speeds up the proposal process with a knowledge base of past proposals.

### Hypermod

Difficulty: 10/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-hypermod-homepage-1024x520.png)

**What it does:** [**Helps developers handle large-scale code migrations**](https://www.hypermod.io/), reducing downtime and flagging vulnerabilities.

**Benefit:** Ensures a clean codebase post-migration.

### AICallCenter

Difficulty: 6/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-aicalcentre-homepage-1024x501.png)

**What it does:** [**Automates phone calls**](https://aicallcenter.co/) for interviews, surveys, and appointment setting using AI.

**Benefit:** Saves costs compared to traditional outsourcing.

### Inxtruc

Difficulty: 1/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-inxtruc-homepage-1024x485.png)

**What it does:** [**Simplifies the creation of step-by-step guides**](https://inxtruc.com/en) for team members.

**Benefit:** Enhances employee onboarding and sharing knowledge.

### Vvault

Difficulty: 7/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-vvault-homepage-1024x456.png)

**What it does:** [**An AI-powered document scanner**](https://vvoult.com/) and expense tracker that automates data extraction from emails, documents, and images.

**Benefit:** Saves time by eliminating manual data entry and organizing documents quickly.

### Axe AI

Difficulty: 8/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-axi.io-homepage-1024x472.png)

**What it does:** [**Turns the company purchase process into a chat interface**](https://joinaxe.io/), automating approval requests and integrating with task management tools.

**Benefit:** Simplifies purchasing and gives business owners more visibility into spending.

We now go from our favorite market to what we find the fussiest, most insufferable market of all.

## Developers

Maybe we're overgeneralizing, maybe *we're*fussy and insufferable.

Anyways, since these are developer-focused, we have to throw some tech jargon around.

Some markets just have a higher barrier to entry, and you’ve got to be a bit nerdy for these.

### Simple KPI

Difficulty: 7/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-simple-kpi-homepage-1024x622.png)

**What it does:** [**A tool for tracking website or app KPIs**](https://www.simplekpi.com/) and providing real-time dashboards.

**Benefit:** Helps developers act on feedback rather than spending time gathering it.

### Supabugs

Difficulty: 9/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-supabugs-homepage-1024x484.png)

**What it does:** [**An API-first issue tracker**](https://www.supabugs.io/) that automates bug reporting and integrates with customer support tools.

**Benefit:** Streamlines bug resolution and integrates with existing systems.

### Spling

Difficulty: 3/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-splling-homepage-1024x520.png)

**What it does:** [**A spell-check tool for websites**](https://www.spl.ing/spellcheck), optimizing meta tags for SEO.

**Benefit:** Helps improve link previews and SEO rankings.

### Detachless

Difficulty: 5/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-detachless-homepage-1024x544.png)

**What it does:** A [**Figma extension that enables users to publish websites**](https://detachless.com/) directly from Figma.

**Benefit:** Saves time for solo founders or designer-heavy projects.

### Arkai

Difficulty: 10/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-arkai-homepage-1024x461.png)

**What it does:** [**A no-code AI agent creator**](https://arkai.app/) for users to design and integrate AI agents without writing code.

**Benefit:** Democratizes AI development for those without technical expertise.

### Temporal

Difficulty: 3/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-temporal-homepage-1024x520.png)

**What it does:** A tool for [**managing multi-location employee scheduling**](https://temporal.app/) in one calendar.

**Benefit:** Simplifies scheduling, including holidays, time off, and projects.

### AppReply

Difficulty: 4/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-appreply-homepage-1024x486.png)

**What it does:** [**Automates app store review replies**](https://appreply.co/) using templates and AI.

**Benefit:** Saves time for development teams and helps manage customer feedback.

## Job Seekers

You might be thinking job seekers aren’t a good market because…aren’t they broke?

Probably.

But broke people still have a bit of money, and here are some SaaS ideas to**squeeze** it out of them Andrew Tate style.

### ProRes.ai

Difficulty: 1/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-preresai-homepage-1024x477.png)

**What it does:** [**Uses AI to build resumes**](https://prores.ai/) specifically tailored for job applications.

**Benefit:** Ensures resumes stand out in an increasingly automated hiring landscape.

### Careerific

Difficulty: 2/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-careerific-homepage-1024x598.png)

**What it does:** An [**AI-powered career counselor**](https://www.careerific.com/) that tailors resumes and cover letters for specific roles.

**Benefit:** Helps users make informed career decisions and prepare professional documents.

### hirepath.ai

Difficulty: 2/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-hirepath-homepage-1024x469.png)

**What it does:** Offers [**mock interview preparation**](https://www.hirepath.ai/) based on real job postings.

**Benefit:** Helps job seekers practice and improve their interview skills.

### OfferLanded

Difficulty:**Don’t do this / 10**

![](/images/blog/2025-04-offerlanded-ai-homepage-1024x478.png)

**Benefit:** Boosts confidence during interviews and ensures better responses.

But these feel like they cater to a specific department rather than the whole organization.

So here are some SaaS ideas to help marketers market and salespeople sell.

### SocLeads

Difficulty: 4/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-socleads-homepage-1024x547.png)

**What it does:** [**Scrapes social media platforms for emails**](https://socleads.com/) to generate leads.

**Benefit:** Expands outreach efforts for marketing teams.

### Fireshort

Difficulty: 7/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-fireshorts-homepage-1024x563.png)

**What it does:** AI-powered [**tool that transforms long-form videos into short-form content**](https://www.fireshort.in/) for platforms like TikTok and Instagram.

**Benefit:** Simplifies content creation and maximizes engagement.

### PostPenguin

Difficulty: 1/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-postpenguin-homepage-1024x517.png)

**What it does:** [**AI tool that generates social media posts**](https://postpenguin.com/) optimized for different platforms.

**Benefit:** Saves time for marketing teams needing to produce a high volume of content.

### WaitListKit

Difficulty: 3/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-waitlistkit-homepage-1024x547.png)

**What it does:** [**A no-code landing page builder for validating SaaS ideas**](https://www.waitlistkit.com/) with waitlist forms and email storage.

### LinkWall

Difficulty: 2/10

![](/images/blog/2025-04-linkwall-homepage-1024x488.png)
