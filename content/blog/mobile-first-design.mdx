As of September 2024, [**mobile devices account for 61.72% of global internet usage**](https://gs.statcounter.com/platform-market-share/desktop-mobile-tablet), far surpassing desktop (36.29%) and tablet (1.98%). 

![](/images/blog/2024-10-a-young-person-teaching-an-old-user-how-to-use-a-smartphone-to-show-how-diverse-mobile-users-are-1024x683.jpg)

With mobile dominating the market, it's crucial for businesses to prioritize mobile-first design in [**app development**](https://upstackstudio.com/blog/app-development/). 

## What is Mobile First Design?

Mobile First Design is a strategy where the [**app design and development process**](https://upstackstudio.com/blog/app-development-lifecycle/) starts with the smallest screen - typically mobile devices.

It then scales up to larger screens like tablets and desktops. 

![What is Mobile First Design?](/images/blog/2024-10-the-same-app-on-a-phone-and-laptop-screen-to-show-impotance-of-having-cross-platform-applications-with-good-mobile-UI-1024x647.jpg)

This design philosophy ensures that apps are optimized for mobile users, who constitute the majority of internet traffic. 

The Mobile First approach allows developers to focus on essential features and functionalities, simplifying the user experience (UX) and enhancing performance on mobile devices.
