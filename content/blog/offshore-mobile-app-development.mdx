### Can You Afford Local Developers? 

Developers aren’t like angry girlfriends who give the silent treatment, so you just have to ask!

![picture of unhappy couple as a joke about offshore mobile app developers and business owners breaking up](/images/blog/2025-05-picture-of-unhappy-couple-as-a-joke-about-offshore-mobile-app-developers-and-business-owners-breaking-up-1024x683.jpg)

Reach out to qualified local app development agencies and they’ll usually send you a form or email asking for key details about your mobile app idea, like:

- Its target audience
- Core features
- Competitors you know of

If it’s something they can take on, they’ll come back with a rough time and cost estimate.

Repeat this with several more local agencies and after five agencies get back to you, you’ll have a pretty accurate range of what your app costs to build locally.

But wait, you might (should!) be thinking:

*“What if they say yes just to get the job even if they suck?”*

A valid concern.

That’s why we said ***qualified*agencies–and the qualifying comes from you! 

Instead of spamming emails to 50 app agencies you don’t know, take the time to go through their reviews and portfolios to verify they have solid client feedback and experience.

![](/images/blog/2025-05-review-of-upstack-studio-by-a-client-for-app-development-project.png)

Bonus points if they’ve built similar mobile apps to what you have in mind.

This will take significantly more time, but it’s time well spent.

And like we said, once you have five quotes, you’ll know how much your app costs to build locally.

If you can’t afford it, time to cast a wider net and go offshore.

## How to Hire Offshore App Developers

The one real difference between hiring an offshore or local app developer is the level of risk.

**With offshore developers, you have zero legal recourse if they breach a contract.**

And so, working with offshore talent means accepting you might lose your money without so much as a home screen to show for it, even if you sign**[ the most comprehensive app development NDA in the world](https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&rct=j&q=&esrc=s&source=web&cd=&cad=rja&uact=8&ved=2ahUKEwjm0pf-qLGNAxWrsFYBHYbMMNEQFnoECCAQAQ&url=https%3A%2F%2Fupstackstudio.com%2Fblog%2Fnda-app-development%2F&usg=AOvVaw3t48EM44XhrN90SD_zfrXT&opi=89978449)**.
