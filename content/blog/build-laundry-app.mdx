## Why DobiQueen as the Gold Standard for Laundry Apps?

DobiQueen allows users to schedule pickups, pay securely, track their laundry, and receive it on time thanks to a complete ecosystem of digital solutions.

### 1. Guest Access Web App

DobiQueen allows customers to book laundry services on their**[web app](https://upstackstudio.com/blog/mobile-web-vs-web-application/)** as guests, eliminating the need for account creation.

![dobiqueen use app without creating account](/images/blog/2024-11-dobiqueen-web-app.png)

This feature makes it convenient for new or occasional users to place orders quickly and easily.

### 2. DobiQueen is Available as Mobile Apps on Android and iOS

With**[dedicated mobile apps on both Android and iOS,](https://upstackstudio.com/blog/native-vs-hybrid-app-development/)** DobiQueen provides a seamless and accessible experience across devices.

![dobiqueen mobile app](/images/blog/2024-11-dobiqueen-android-and-ios-apps.png)

This approach maximizes convenience for users, encouraging them to place orders whenever needed.

### 3. B2B Targeted Outreach

DobiQueen strategically targets business clients, including gyms, homestays, and Airbnb hosts, through a focused laundry service B2B program. 

![dobiqueen laundry service b2b program](/images/blog/2024-11-dobiqueen-b2b-verticals-1024x443.png)

By catering to these high-volume users, DobiQueen secures a consistent stream of orders from large-scale clients, strengthening its market presence.

And of course, we can’t forget the real reason DobiQueen is able to leverage digitization so effectively.

### 4. DobiQueen Has Nearly 80 Locations

DobiQueen was established in 2015 and has grown to [**over 80 DobiQueen locations**](https://www.dobiqueen.my/stores) across the Klang Valley, with presence in every major township and district.

![dobiqueen locations](/images/blog/2024-11-news-article-of-dobiqueen-founder-expanding-to-over-80-locations.png)

This allows them to cover a wide geographic area, improving availability for customers.

Furthermore, their large-scale operation helps them benefit from economies of scale, reducing operational costs and allowing them to offer competitive pricing.

Of course, it doesn’t mean you too need a business as established as theirs–that’s why partnerships exist!

## Key Features of a Good Laundry App

A laundry app must support four distinct user interfaces—**Customer**,**Service Provider**,**Delivery**, and**Admin**—each with essential features that make the app functional and user-friendly.

Here's a breakdown of the key features for each interface:

### Customer-Facing App

-**Customer Registration**: Allow login via social media, email, or phone number.

-**Search Nearby Laundry**: Find nearby service providers based on ratings and reviews.

-**Laundry Type Selection**: Choose from services like cleaning, ironing, etc.

-**Cost Calculation**: Show estimated costs and breakdown for each item.

-**Laundry Order Scheduling**: Choose a convenient time and location for pickup and delivery.

-**Order Tracking**: Real-time status updates on laundry order.

-**In-App Chat**: Communicate with the delivery person or laundry service for details.

-**Payment**: Multiple payment options, including COD, credit/debit card, and mobile wallets.

-**Push Notifications**: Stay updated on order status, pickup/delivery schedules, and offers.

-**Reviews and Ratings**: Provide feedback on the service received.

### Service Provider App

-**Profile Management**: Create and manage the laundry shop profile with details and contact info.

-**Price Management**: Set and adjust prices for various laundry services.

-**Earnings and Commission**: View earnings and commissions from orders.

-**Order Management**: Track and manage orders in progress, pending, or delivered.

-**Offers and Discount Management**: Launch discounts, offers, and coupon codes to attract customers.

-**Review Management**: Monitor customer feedback and address negative reviews.

### Delivery App

-**View Pickup Orders**: Access and manage upcoming laundry pickups.

-**View Delivery Orders**: See orders for delivery with specific instructions.

-**Order History**: Review past orders for reference.

-**In-App Navigation**: Find optimal routes for pickups and deliveries.

-**View Earnings**: Track daily, weekly, and monthly earnings from deliveries.

-**Payment Collection**: Collect payments from customers who prefer cash.

-**Push Notifications**: Get notified about new order requests.

-**Order Management**: Accept or reject orders based on availability.

### Admin Panel

-**Laundry Category Management**: Manage different types of laundry services offered.

-**User Management**: Access and manage customer data and orders.

-**Launderer Management**: Control which launders are listed and the services they provide.

-**Commission Management**: Set commission rates for different laundry services.

-**Push Notification Management**: Customize and manage notifications to customers.

-**Marketing Campaigns Management**: Create targeted marketing campaigns for different customer segments.

-**Reporting and Analytics**: Generate reports to analyze service performance and improve user experience.

Of course, you don’t have to start with everything right out of the gate–read up on the concept of a Minimum Lovable Product to see how you can start small but still make a big impact.
