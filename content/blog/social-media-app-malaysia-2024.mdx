## Social Media User Demographics in Malaysia

As of 2024, 28.68 million Malaysians - 83% of the population - are active users of social media. For contrast, only[** 49.9% of Indonesians are social media users**](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2024-indonesia#:~:text=The%20number%20of%20social%20media,on%20data%20to%20learn%20why).) and even beats [**Singapore’s 85% penetration rate**](https://www.meltwater.com/en/blog/social-media-statistics-singapore) in the same period.

The astute business owner will want to know platform specific numbers, so let’s get to that!

## Facebook

![](/images/blog/2024-03-facebook-logo-to-show-company-is-using-react-native.png)

**User Statistics:** Facebook had 22.35 million users in Malaysia at the start of 2024, covering 64.8% of the total population. Notably, Facebook’s ad reach was 66.5% of the local internet user base. The platform saw a growth of 10.4% (+2.1 million users) over the past year.

**Gender Distribution:** 44.8% of Facebook’s ad audience was female, with 55.2% male.

**Recent Trends:** Despite a 1.8% decrease in ad reach from October 2023 to January 2024, Facebook remains a key player in Malaysia's social media landscape.

## YouTube

![](/images/blog/2024-06-Logo_of_YouTube.png)

**User Statistics:** YouTube reached 24.10 million users, or 69.9% of Malaysia's total population. This reach equates to 71.7% of the internet user base.

**Gender Distribution:** The platform's ad audience was 48.1% female and 51.9% male.

**Recent Trends:** YouTube’s ad reach increased by 0.8% (+200,000 users) over the past year, showing stable growth.

## Instagram

![](/images/blog/2024-03-instagram-logo-to-show-a-big-social-media-company-using-react-native.png)

**User Statistics:** Instagram had 15.70 million users in early 2024, representing 45.5% of the total population. This figure translates to 46.7% of the local internet user base.

**Gender Distribution:** 56.0% of Instagram’s ad audience was female, with 44.0% male.

**Recent Trends:** Instagram's ad reach grew by 12.9% (+1.8 million users) from January 2023 to January 2024.

## TikTok

![](/images/blog/2024-09-TikTok_logo-1024x299.png)

**User Statistics:** TikTok’s ad reach was notable, with 28.68 million users aged 18 and above, reflecting 114% of the adult population. Overall, TikTok reached 85.4% of Malaysia’s internet users.

**Gender Distribution:** 44.3% of TikTok’s ad audience was female, and 55.7% male.

**Recent Trends:** TikTok saw substantial growth with a 48.6% increase in ad reach (+9.4 million users) over the past year.

## Facebook Messenger

![](/images/blog/2024-06-Facebook_Messenger_logo.png)

**User Statistics:** Facebook Messenger reached 11.85 million users in Malaysia, covering 34.4% of the total population. This figure is 35.3% of the local internet user base.

**Gender Distribution:** 43.9% of Messenger’s ad audience was female, with 56.1% male.

**Recent Trends:** Messenger’s ad reach increased by 5.8% (+650,000 users) from January 2023 to January 2024.

## LinkedIn

![](/images/blog/2024-05-linkedin-logo.png)

**User Statistics:** LinkedIn had 7.80 million members in Malaysia, which equates to 22.6% of the total population. This reach is 23.2% of the local internet user base.

**Gender Distribution:** 42.6% of LinkedIn’s ad audience was female, and 57.4% male.

**Recent Trends:** LinkedIn’s ad reach grew by 11.4% (+800,000 users) over the past year.

## Snapchat

![](/images/blog/2024-09-snapchat-logo.png)

**User Statistics:** Snapchat had 1.46 million users in Malaysia, representing 4.2% of the total population. This figure translates to 4.4% of the local internet user base.

**Gender Distribution:** Snapchat’s ad audience was 50.2% female and 47.8% male.

**Recent Trends:** Snapchat saw a minor increase of 0.9% (+13,000 users) in its ad reach over the past year.

## X (Twitter)

![](/images/blog/2024-02-twitter-logo-to-show-popular-social-media-platform-built-on-ruby-on-rails-1024x1024.png)

**User Statistics:** X had 5.71 million users in Malaysia, covering 16.5% of the total population. This reach is 17.0% of the local internet user base.

**Gender Distribution:** 43.8% of X’s ad audience was female, while 56.2% was male.

**Recent Trends:** X’s ad reach increased by 3.8% (+208,000 users) from January 2023 to January 2024, though it experienced a 7.9% decrease in ad reach between October 2023 and January 2024.
